About the Project
What is Poshan Abhiyan?
Poshan Abhiyaan was launched in 2018 to improve nutrition for children, pregnant women, and lactating mothers. Ministry of Women and Child Development implements the mission through Anganwadi centres, which provide nutrition, health education, and supplementary feeding. This digital system helps Anganwadi workers manage records, track ration distribution, monitor attendance, and generate reports efficiently.
Est. 8 March 2018 MoWCD · Govt. of India 1000-Day Mission Bhuvan M Gouda
